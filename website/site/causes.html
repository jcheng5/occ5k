The Causes

All proceeds from the race will benefit two non-profit organizations, one local and one international, that have demonstrated tremendous effectiveness in dealing with HIV/AIDS.

REACH Ministries

REACH Ministries of Tacoma, Washington specializes in care for children and families who have been affected by HIV/AIDS. They have recently expanded their services to meet the needs of those in 12 states across the U.S. As part of its program, REACH offers an annual camp experience which provides a safe haven for families free from financial obligation and social threats. For more information on how you can further support and participate in their work, please visit www.reachministries.org.

Watch a short video introduction to REACH.

Living Hope

Based in Cape Town, South Africa, Living Hope Community Centre offers medical care and prevention programs to thousands annually. Working primarily in slum communities throughout the Cape Town region, Living Hope takes a holistic approach to addressing the issues created by HIV/AIDS by employing nationals who serve as life skills educators, home-based care workers, counselors, and medical professionals. For more information about Living Hope's work and how you can support it, visit www.livinghope.co.za.
