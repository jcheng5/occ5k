About Overlake

OCC is a place where people come as they are, not a place for people who have it all together. No perfect people allowed! If you have any questions, we'd be happy to help you out in any way we can. If you can join us this Sunday, please know you can relax, grab a cup of complimentary coffee before the service and feel free to participate in as much or as little as you want. Check out occ.org and we'll see you on Sunday!
