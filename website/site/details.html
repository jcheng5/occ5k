Race Information

  • The race route begins and ends at OCC
  • Kids Fun Run will begin at 8:45am, just prior to the start of the 5K
  • Event will occur regardless of weather conditions
  • Strollers and wagons are welcome (WALKING ONLY)
  • No bicycles, tricycles, skateboards or wheelies
  • No pets, please
  • Medical personnel will be on-site
  • Water will be available on race route
  • Food and drink will be available post-race
  • To guarantee an event t-shirt, you must register by October 4
  • T-shirts not picked up by 11AM on race day will be forfeited
  • Sorry, no refunds
